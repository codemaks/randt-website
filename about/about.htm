About

The Club meets on Thursdays from 7:30pm at the Scout Hut, Seymour Gardens, Twickenham TW1 3AR.  This is the home venue for the Thames Valley League A, B and D teams.  Please do not use the club venues for postal correspondence.

Facilities for disabled people are excellent.

The annual subscription is £40, with discount for prompt payment. All club members are required to be members of the English Chess Federation (any category).

The building is about a 5 to 10 minute walk from Twickenham Railway station and has very good bus links.

Map of Scout Hall Twickenham

The Thames Valley League C team play home matches on Tuesday evenings at the Twickenham Club in Church Street, Twickenham, TW1 3NJ.
