About

The Club meets on Thursdays from 7:30pm at the Christ Church Hall, Station Road, Teddington, TW11 9AA.  This is the home venue for the Thames Valley League A, B and D teams.  Please do not use the club venues for postal correspondence.

The hall is located at the rear of the left hand side of the church. Please note: access to the church hall is via the marked gate on Christchurch Avenue.

Facilities for disabled people are excellent.

The annual subscription is £40, with discount for prompt payment. All club members are required to be members of the English Chess Federation (any category).

The building is situated close to Teddington Railway station and has very good bus links.

Map of Christ Church Hall Teddington

The Thames Valley League C team play home matches on Tuesday evenings at the Twickenham Club in Church Street, Twickenham, TW1 3NJ.
