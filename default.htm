This is the home of Richmond and Twickenham Chess Club. Please follow the links to find out about us. If you live in the area and you want to play chess then you are welcome. We cater for all levels from beginner to expert. If you want to improve your chess playing ability with competitive games against strong players or just play a few games in a friendly atmosphere then we have a lot to offer you.

In the 2016-2017 season we are running one team in the London League and five teams in the Thames Valley League and also teams in the Eastman Cup and Thames Valley League Knockout competition.
