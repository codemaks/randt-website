Club Internal Tournament

Anyone who is new to the game, a bit rusty or just wants to come down to play some games against less experienced players, the Richmond & Twickenham Chess Club has an Internal Tournament that is specifically for lower level players.  Nette Robinson runs this and would be delighted to hear from you.  Either email or call Nette:  (01932) 780 657 or 07876 755 114 to find out more.
