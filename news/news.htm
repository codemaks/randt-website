News

28 September 2015: Fixtures for the new season are now available.

12 September 2015: The club AGM was held on 3 September 2015 and the club is now open for the season. Fixtures will be available in the near future.

26 August 2014: The 2014-2015 season will be starting soon. The first fixtures have been published and will be made available shortly.

31 May 2014: The Thames Valley League knockout team failed to defend the title they won last year, losing 3½-2½ to Surbiton in the semi-final.

28 March 2014: The London League team has won the Eastman Cup, defeating Cavendish 7-3 in the final yesterday. Well done to all involved. It is some measure of revenge for narrowly losing the final to the same opposition in three of the previous four seasons.

The club wishes its president Abraham Neviazsky a very happy eightieth birthday. Two of his may games for the club can be found linked on the games page.

15 March 2014: The club now has a new logo. Please see the site front page.

6 September 2013: The website is the process of being update for the start of the 2013-2014 season. Fixture lists will be available shortly.
